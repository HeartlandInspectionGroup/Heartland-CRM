Inspection Services & Specialty Inspections

A pre-purchase inspection is conducted before you buy a home to identify existing problems and potential issues. This comprehensive evaluation helps you understand what you're buying and can be used as a negotiating tool with the seller.

Starting at $375, this inspection covers all major systems and components of the home and typically takes 2-4 hours depending on the property size.

Yes, pre-listing inspections help sellers identify and address issues before listing, price their home accurately, and negotiate from a position of strength. It can lead to faster sales and fewer surprises during the buyer's inspection.

Sellers who get pre-listing inspections often see smoother transactions and can market their home with confidence.

New construction inspections are performed at various stages during the building process - foundation, framing, pre-drywall, pre-closing, and 11-month warranty periods. These inspections ensure quality standards are met and code compliance is maintained.

We offer individual phase inspections starting at $350 each, or a complete package that saves you $300 when you bundle all phases together.

Radon is a colorless, odorless radioactive gas that naturally occurs in soil and can seep into homes. It's the leading cause of lung cancer among non-smokers, responsible for approximately 21,000 deaths annually according to the EPA.

Testing is the only way to know if dangerous levels are present in a home. Our professional radon testing service ($150) uses EPA-approved methods and provides accurate results within 48-72 hours.

Consider a mold inspection if you notice musty odors, visible mold growth, water damage, or if you or family members experience unexplained allergies or respiratory issues. Mold can hide behind walls, under floors, and in HVAC systems.

Our mold inspection starts at $300 and includes visual inspection, moisture detection, and recommendations. Air quality testing and thermal imaging are available for an additional fee.

Annual maintenance inspections ($295) are preventive checkups for homeowners who want to catch small problems before they become expensive repairs. We evaluate your home's major systems and provide a prioritized maintenance plan.

Think of it as a yearly physical for your home - identifying issues early can save thousands of dollars in emergency repairs down the road.

We use EPA-approved continuous radon monitors that provide highly accurate readings. The devices are placed in the lowest lived-in level of the home for 48-72 hours, monitoring radon levels continuously throughout the test period.

Results show both short-term levels and average concentrations, giving you a clear picture of radon presence in the home.

If radon levels exceed the EPA action level of 4.0 pCi/L, we recommend radon mitigation. This typically involves installing a system that vents radon gas from beneath the foundation to the outside air.

While we don't perform mitigation ourselves, we can recommend qualified radon mitigation professionals. The good news is that radon issues can be effectively resolved with proper mitigation.
