Introduction

Heartland Home Inspections is more than a service—it's a promise to families across Roscoe, Illinois and surrounding communities. Our brand represents trust, thoroughness, and the dedication that comes from treating every home inspection as if it were for our own family.

These guidelines ensure our brand maintains consistency and professionalism across all touchpoints, from our website to business cards, vehicle wraps to social media presence.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, honest home inspections that empower families to make informed decisions about the biggest investment of their lives. We believe in thorough work, transparent communication, and building lasting relationships with our community.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family

We're a family business serving families. Every inspection is performed with the care and attention we'd give to our own loved ones' homes.

🤝 Trust

Our reputation is built on honest assessments, clear communication, and always putting the client's best interests first.

💪 Hard Work

We don't cut corners. Every inspection is thorough, detailed, and delivered with the professionalism you deserve.

Logo Design

The Heartland Home Inspections logo combines a stylized home with a magnifying glass—symbolizing both the comfort of home and the thoroughness of our inspections. The design is clean, professional, and easily recognizable across all applications.

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Clear Space: Always maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height of the house in the logo. No other graphic elements, text, or edges should enter this space.

Minimum Size:

  • Print: 0.75 inches (19mm) in height
  • Digital: 48 pixels in height
  • Social Media Profile: 200x200 pixels minimum

Logo Usage Guidelines

✓ Do

  • Use approved logo variations only
  • Maintain proper clear space
  • Scale proportionally
  • Use on appropriate backgrounds
  • Ensure minimum size requirements

✗ Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change logo colors
  • Add effects (shadows, glows)
  • Rotate the logo
  • Place on busy backgrounds

Typography

Our typography system uses two complementary fonts: Crimson Pro for headlines and Work Sans for body text. This combination creates a professional yet approachable feel.

Crimson Pro (Headlines & Display)

Heartland Home Inspections

Professional Home Inspections

Family, Trust, Hard Work

Usage: Headlines, subheadings, logo lockup, quotes, and emphasis text

Weights: Regular (400), Semibold (600), Bold (700)

Work Sans (Body & Interface)

Navigation & Buttons

Body text should be clear, readable, and professional. Work Sans provides excellent legibility across all digital and print applications while maintaining a modern, friendly appearance.

Supporting text and captions

Usage: Body copy, navigation, buttons, forms, captions

Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500), Semibold (600), Bold (700)
